About Me

I am a researcher and data scientist with a background in civil engineering, environmental engineering, and geodesy. My work focuses on Earth observation, satellite hydrology, machine learning, time series analysis, and uncertainty-aware modeling.

I have worked extensively with satellite altimetry, GRACE observations, river discharge estimation, total water storage analysis, and geospatial data products. I am especially interested in translating these quantitative and scientific skills into real-world applications in risk, hazard, and insurance analytics.
